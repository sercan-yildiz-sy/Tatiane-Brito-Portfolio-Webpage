Quickly

A smart pantry management app that tracks your inventory in real time and updates automatically as you cook. It suggests personalized recipes, generates meal plans, and creates a dynamic shopping list to simplify your kitchen routine.

2021

UX Research and UX/UI Design

GOAL

This project aims to reduce food waste and decision fatigue by helping families organize their pantry, easily plan meals, and shop smarter through automated inventory management. All in a concise and user-friendly mobile app.

Figma

Illustrator

Kick-Off

This project began with the challenge of tackling a common household pain point: the lack of organization in pantry inventory, which often leads to food waste, confusion, and fatigue when preparing meals. The kick-off focused on understanding the everyday struggles of families in managing their kitchens.

Process:

Empathize

For the first step, a desk-research was conducted and survey insights were identified and categorized. Followed by a user persona development.

Define

As part of the define stage, where the goal was to explore the problem and identify possible opportunities, two studies were conducted: a journey map and competitive audit.

Ideate

Once opportunities were mapped, we move on to ideating, where we turn them into ideas for solutions. Here the main goals were to create a user flow, design system and wireframes with task flow.

Prototype & Testing

After the LO-FI prototype a user test was conducted to identify improvement points and reach the HI-FI prototype.
