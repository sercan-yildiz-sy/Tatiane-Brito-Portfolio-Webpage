Kick-Off

This project began with a simple goal: to reimagine the brand’s identity so it could reflect its growth, values, and vision for the future. It had to serve as a guideline for all creatives in the company, allowing the digital media to showcase and reinforce the same identity.

To kick-off the process, apart from aligning goals with stakeholders, we needed to create an identity that spoke to the target audience while differentiating the company from competitors. The goal was to create something modern, authentic, simple and trustworthy.
