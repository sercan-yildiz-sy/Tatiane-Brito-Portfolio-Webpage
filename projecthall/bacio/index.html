Bacio di Latte

Bacio di Latte is a family-owned company, originally founded by brothers Edoardo and Luigi Tonolli in 2011 in São Paulo, Brazil. Offering a range of products, such as gelatos, coffee and retail items.

GOAL

The goal of this educational project was to suggest an onboarding experience for the brand, consisting of one landing page.

Understanding

From a website analysis to social media, in the understanding stage the goal was to map how the brand presents itself.

UI Elements

From the analysis, a UI system was created to maintain consistency.

Wireframes

Prototype

Finally with a layout to follow and a system to apply, the landing page was created.
